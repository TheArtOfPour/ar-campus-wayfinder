Campus Wayfinder

Select a location on the map or search to find buildings and points of interest.

About this App

This AR Campus Wayfinder uses LocAR.js and A-Frame to display points of interest based on your GPS location.

How to use:

  1. Allow access to camera, GPS, and orientation sensors when prompted
  2. Look around to see POIs in the real world
  3. Use the search bar to find specific locations
  4. Select a location to focus on it

Tips:

  • Make sure GPS is enabled and you have a strong signal
  • Calibrate your device's compass if POIs appear offset
  • The app works best in open areas with clear sky visibility
